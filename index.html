Gentse Feesten Duplicate

This school assignment had as focus responsive design and dynamic content. With every refresh of the page, a different picture gets loaded. On different sizes of screens the menu changes to fit it properly. All data is fetched fom an external API. I personally loved this project because I took the time to explore accessibility. From alt text for images, to skip to main link to the world of aria-labels, it took me to interesting places. Trying to make my corner of the web more accessible.

Let's do it

This is a simple application where you can add categories and add, delete and change to-do items. You can share categories with other users, using a valid e-mail address and you can add a deadline date. Easily change to do items to done. This app uses authentication, validation and has a database.

Logo Design

A car insurance company wanted an easy to recognise new logo to go with his new website. I went with a shield, symbolizing security, protection. In the shield you can see a road shaped in the letter R and the shield shapes a V for the name of the insurance.
